Hasio Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 12, 2026

Welcome to Hasio ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Hasio - Al-Ahsa Oasis Guide.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Full name (optional)
  • Account type (user, business, or provider)
  • Authentication data from Google or Apple Sign-In (if used)

1.2 User-Generated Content

We collect content you create within the app:

  • Moments (photos and notes you save)
  • Day plans and itineraries
  • Favorites and saved places
  • Chat messages with our AI assistant

1.3 Voice Data

Important: When you use the Voice Assistant feature, your voice recordings are sent to third-party services for processing. This includes Groq for speech-to-text transcription and ElevenLabs for text-to-speech responses. By using the Voice Assistant, you consent to this data processing.

1.4 Device Information

We may collect:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Language preferences

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

2.1 Clerk

We use Clerk for user authentication. Your account information (email, name, authentication credentials) is securely managed by Clerk's authentication platform.

Privacy Policy: https://clerk.com/legal/privacy

2.2 Convex

We use Convex for database storage and real-time data synchronization. Your account information, moments, plans, favorites, and app-generated content are stored securely on Convex's cloud infrastructure.

Privacy Policy: https://www.convex.dev/privacy

2.3 Groq

When you use the Voice Assistant, your voice recordings are sent to Groq's Whisper API for speech-to-text transcription. Your text conversations are processed by Groq's LLaMA model to generate AI responses.

Privacy Policy: https://groq.com/privacy-policy/

2.4 ElevenLabs

AI-generated responses are sent to ElevenLabs to convert text to natural speech for the Voice Assistant feature.

Privacy Policy: https://elevenlabs.io/privacy

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Hasio app
  • Process your authentication and manage your account
  • Store and display your moments, plans, and favorites
  • Provide AI-powered travel assistance
  • Enable the Voice Assistant feature
  • Improve our services and user experience

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You can delete your account at any time through the app settings, which will permanently remove:

  • Your profile information
  • All moments and photos you've uploaded
  • Your day plans and saved items
  • Your favorites list

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time by going to Settings > Delete Account in the app. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

5.2 Voice Data Consent

Before using the Voice Assistant for the first time, you will be asked to consent to voice data processing. If you decline, you can still use the text-based chat features.

5.3 Access and Correction

You can access and update your profile information through the app at any time.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication through Clerk
  • Row-level security policies on our Convex database
  • Industry-standard encryption for data at rest

7. Children's Privacy

Hasio is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
